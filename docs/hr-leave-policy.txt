LEAVE POLICY
============

1. Sick Leave
   Full-time employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year. Sick leave may be used
   for the employee's own illness, medical appointments, or to care for an immediate family
   member. A doctor's note is required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days. Unused
   sick days do not carry over to the next year and are not paid out upon termination.

2. Parental Leave
   - Birth parents: 16 weeks of paid parental leave at 100% of base salary.
   - Non-birth parents (including adoptive and foster parents): 8 weeks of paid parental
     leave at 100% of base salary.
   Parental leave must be taken within 12 months of the birth, adoption, or foster placement.
   Employees may request an additional 4 weeks of unpaid leave subject to manager approval.

3. Bereavement Leave
   Employees are entitled to paid bereavement leave as follows:
   - Spouse/domestic partner, child, parent: 5 days
   - Sibling, grandparent, grandchild: 3 days
   - Extended family (aunt, uncle, cousin, in-law): 1 day
   Additional unpaid leave may be requested through the employee's manager.

4. Jury Duty
   Employees summoned for jury duty will receive their full regular pay for up to 10 business
   days. If jury duty extends beyond 10 days, the company will pay the difference between
   jury compensation and regular pay for up to an additional 20 days.

5. Voting Leave
   Employees are allowed up to 2 hours of paid time off to vote in federal, state, or local
   elections if their work schedule does not allow sufficient time outside working hours.

6. Military Leave
   The company complies with USERRA and provides up to 15 days of paid military leave per
   year for employees who are members of the National Guard or Reserves. Benefits continue
   during military leave as required by law.

7. Personal Leave of Absence
   Employees with at least 1 year of service may request an unpaid personal leave of absence
   for up to 30 days. Approval is at the discretion of the department head and HR. Benefits
   may be continued during personal leave at the employee's expense.

8. Holiday Schedule
   The company observes the following paid holidays:
   - New Year's Day
   - Martin Luther King Jr. Day
   - Presidents' Day
   - Memorial Day
   - Independence Day
   - Labor Day
   - Thanksgiving Day
   - Day after Thanksgiving
   - Christmas Eve
   - Christmas Day
   Additionally, employees receive 2 floating holidays per year to use at their discretion.
