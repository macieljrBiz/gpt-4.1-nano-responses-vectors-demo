EMPLOYEE BENEFITS POLICY
========================

1. Health Insurance
   All full-time employees are eligible for health insurance coverage starting on the first day
   of the month following 30 days of employment. The company offers three plan options:
   - Basic Plan: Covers essential medical services with a $500 annual deductible.
   - Standard Plan: Covers medical, dental, and vision with a $250 annual deductible.
   - Premium Plan: Comprehensive coverage including mental health, chiropractic, and
     alternative medicine with a $100 annual deductible.
   The company covers 80% of the premium for the employee and 50% for dependents.

2. Retirement Plan (401k)
   Employees are eligible to participate in the company 401(k) plan after 90 days of employment.
   The company matches employee contributions dollar-for-dollar up to 4% of the employee's
   annual salary. Vesting schedule:
   - Year 1: 25% vested
   - Year 2: 50% vested
   - Year 3: 75% vested
   - Year 4+: 100% vested

3. Paid Time Off (PTO)
   PTO accrual rates are based on years of service:
   - 0-2 years: 15 days per year
   - 3-5 years: 20 days per year
   - 6-10 years: 25 days per year
   - 10+ years: 30 days per year
   PTO can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.

4. Education Assistance
   The company offers tuition reimbursement up to $5,250 per calendar year for approved
   courses related to the employee's current role or career development within the company.
   Employees must maintain a grade of B or higher and remain employed for at least 12 months
   after completing the course.

5. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
   All employees and their immediate family members have access to confidential counseling
   and support services at no cost. Services include mental health support, financial
   counseling, legal consultation, and work-life balance resources. Up to 6 free sessions
   per issue per year.

6. Wellness Program
   The company sponsors a wellness program that includes:
   - Gym membership subsidy of up to $50/month
   - Annual health screenings
   - Flu vaccination clinics
   - Wellness challenges with prizes
   Employees who complete the annual health assessment receive a $200 wellness credit.
